# Can I Put My Own Ads on My Website?

**Category:** Websites and PHP

---

**Absolutely!** Your website is your own, and you can put almost any content you want on it.

As long as the content is not illegal or harmful, you're free to display whatever content you want—including advertisements.

## Things to Keep in Mind

- **You are responsible** for the content displayed on your site.
- Use **safe ads from reputable ad networks** (like Google AdSense).
- Make sure ads comply with your ad network's terms of service.

## Summary

| Question | Answer |
|----------|--------|
| Can I display my own ads? | ✓ Yes |
| Can I use Google AdSense? | ✓ Yes |
| Can I use other ad networks? | ✓ Yes |
| Are there restrictions? | Only for illegal or harmful content |
