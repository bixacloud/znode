# How to Delete Your Znode Client Area Profile

If you want to completely leave Znode and have all your details deleted, you can delete your client area profile yourself.

---

## Requirements

To delete your profile, you **cannot have any hosting accounts**. All hosting accounts must be completely deletedâ€”deactivating is not enough!

See: [How to delete a hosting account](How%20to%20delete%20a%20hosting%20account.md)

---

## What Happens When You Delete Your Profile

| Component | What Happens |
|-----------|--------------|
| Client area profile | Deactivated immediately; completely removed within 48 hours |
| Forum profile | Deactivated and anonymized |
| Forum posts/topics | Remain, but username, email, and profile image are replaced with anonymous placeholders |

---

## How to Delete Your Profile

1. Log in to the client area
2. Go to **Profile** in the main menu
3. Click the **Edit Profile** button
4. Scroll down to the **Delete Profile** section
5. Enter your password in the **Current Password** field
6. Click the **Delete Profile** button

After this, you'll be logged out immediately. Your profile will be completely deleted within 48 hours.

---

## Important Notes

- This action is **permanent** and cannot be undone
- Make sure you've backed up any data you need before deleting
- Delete all hosting accounts first, or you won't be able to delete your profile
