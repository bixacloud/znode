# Can I Contact Support for Script Help?

**Category:** Websites and PHP

---

Znode does not include direct customer support. This means we don't provide any email address, phone number, contact form, or chat system where you can ask for help.

## Your Responsibility

Znode provides a website hosting service. **It's your responsibility** to build a website that works on our hosting service.

## Where to Get Help

If you need help with your website, you can ask your question on the **Hosting Support** category on our community forum. Other users and staff may be able to help you.

### Helpful Resources

| Resource | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| Community Forum | Ask questions and get help from the community |
| Knowledge Base | Browse articles for common issues |
| Search Engine | Search for solutions to specific error messages |

## Tips for Getting Help

1. **Search first:** Your question may have already been answered.
2. **Be specific:** Include error messages, steps you've taken, and what you expected to happen.
3. **Provide context:** Mention which software or scripts you're using.
